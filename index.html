CAS by EducateNXT is a comprehensive software solution designed specifically for educational institutions to manage various administrative and academic tasks efficiently.

CAS offers a range of features including student management, faculty management, attendance tracking, examination management, timetable scheduling, fee management, library management, hostel management, and more.

CAS streamlines administrative processes, enhances communication between stakeholders, improves data accuracy, facilitates better decision-making, and ultimately helps in providing a superior educational experience for students.
